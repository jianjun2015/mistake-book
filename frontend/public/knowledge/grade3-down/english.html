📝 语法总结

1. can 的用法

肯定句：I can hear a bus.
否定句：I can't hear a bus.
疑问句：Can you hear a bus?

2. like 的用法

肯定句：I like cats.
否定句：I don't like cats.
疑问句：Do you like cats?

3. 特殊疑问句

What — 什么：What can you see?
How — 怎么样：How does it feel?
Do — 做：Do you like it?

📝 本学期重点

必背单词

感官动词：hear, see, touch, feel, taste, smell

形容词：soft, hard, rough, smooth, sweet, sour

动物：cat, dog, rabbit, fish, bird

服装：shirt, T-shirt, dress, skirt, shoes

必背句型

  • What can you hear/see? — I can hear/see...
  • How does it feel/taste? — It's soft/sweet.
  • Do you like...? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
  • What shape is it? — It's a circle.
