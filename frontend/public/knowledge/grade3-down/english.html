📝 语法总结

1. be动词的用法

am / is / are 的区别：
  • I am (I'm) — 我是
  • He/She/It is (He's/She's/It's) — 他/她/它是
  • You/We/They are (You're/We're/They're) — 你/我们/他们是

2. like的用法

肯定句：I like cats. (我喜欢猫。)
否定句：I don't like cats. (我不喜欢猫。)
疑问句：Do you like cats? (你喜欢猫吗？)

3. 特殊疑问句

What — 什么：What is this?
What colour — 什么颜色：What colour is it?
How — 怎么样：How does it feel?
Do — 做：Do you like it?

3. 易错点

  • 单复数：What colour is it? vs What colour are they?
  • like后面名词复数：I like cats. (不是 I like cat.)
  • be动词搭配：I am (不是 I is)
